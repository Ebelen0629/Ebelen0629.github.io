North Country Burger

Time: 15 minutes | Serves: 1 | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 beef patty
  • Burger bun
  • Cheese slice
  • Bacon strips
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion
  • Your favorite sauce

Steps

  1. Grill or pan-fry the beef patty.
  2. Toast the bun.
  3. Add cheese on the patty to melt.
  4. Assemble with toppings and sauce.
