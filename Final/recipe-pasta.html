Creamy Garlic Pasta

Time: 20 minutes | Serves: 2 | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pasta (fettuccine or spaghetti)
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream or half & half
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt, pepper, and fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a skillet, melt butter and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add cream and simmer for 3 minutes.
  4. Stir in parmesan cheese until melted.
  5. Toss cooked pasta in the sauce and garnish with parsley.
