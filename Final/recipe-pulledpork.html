Pulled Pork Sandwich

Time: 10 minutes (using pre-cooked pork) | Serves: 2 | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pulled pork
  • 2 burger buns
  • Coleslaw (optional)
  • BBQ sauce

Steps

  1. Warm the pulled pork with BBQ sauce.
  2. Toast the buns lightly.
  3. Pile pork on the bun and top with coleslaw if desired.
