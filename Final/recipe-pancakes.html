Apple Cinnamon Pancakes

Time: 20 minutes | Serves: 2 | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pancake mix
  • 1 apple, diced
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 cup milk
  • Maple syrup for serving

Steps

  1. Mix pancake batter with cinnamon.
  2. Fold in diced apples.
  3. Cook on griddle until golden.
  4. Serve with warm maple syrup.
