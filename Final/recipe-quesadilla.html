Chicken Quesadilla

Time: 15 minutes | Serves: 2 | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1.5 cups shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • 1/2 bell pepper, diced
  • Salsa and sour cream for serving

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Place tortilla down, add cheese, chicken, and peppers.
  3. Top with second tortilla and cook until golden.
  4. Flip and cook the other side.
  5. Cut into wedges and serve with salsa and sour cream.
