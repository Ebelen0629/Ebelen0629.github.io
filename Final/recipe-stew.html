Beef Stew

Time: 45 minutes | Serves: 4 | Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs beef stew meat
  • 4 carrots, chopped
  • 4 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • Salt, pepper, and herbs

Steps

  1. Brown beef in a large pot.
  2. Add onion and cook until soft.
  3. Add carrots, potatoes, broth, and seasoning.
  4. Simmer for 35-40 minutes until tender.
