Veggie Stir-Fry Bowl

Time: 15 minutes | Serves: 2 | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, peppers)
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce or stir-fry sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Garlic and ginger (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and stir-fry garlic and ginger.
  2. Add vegetables and cook until crisp-tender.
  3. Pour in sauce and toss well.
  4. Serve over rice.
