Margherita Flatbread Pizza

Time: 15 minutes | Serves: 2 | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 flatbreads
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil and salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Spread sauce on flatbread.
  3. Top with torn mozzarella and basil.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and bake 8-10 minutes.
