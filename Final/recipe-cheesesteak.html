Classic Philly Cheesesteak

Time: 15 minutes | Serves: 2 | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ribeye or sirloin steak, thinly sliced
  • 4 hoagie rolls
  • 8 slices provolone or American cheese
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • Salt, pepper, and oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a skillet and sauté onions and peppers until soft.
  2. Add thinly sliced steak and cook until browned.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Top with cheese and let it melt.
  5. Serve on toasted hoagie rolls.
