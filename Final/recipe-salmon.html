Salmon Rice Bowl

Time: 20 minutes | Serves: 2 | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup steamed vegetables
  • Soy sauce or teriyaki glaze

Steps

  1. Season and pan-sear salmon 4-5 minutes per side.
  2. Assemble bowls with rice, salmon, avocado, and veggies.
  3. Drizzle with sauce.
