Breakfast Skillet

Time: 20 minutes | Serves: 2 | Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups diced potatoes
  • 1/2 lb breakfast sausage
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • Onion and bell pepper

Steps

  1. Cook potatoes and sausage in a skillet.
  2. Add onions and peppers.
  3. Crack eggs on top and cover until cooked.
  4. Sprinkle cheese and let it melt.
