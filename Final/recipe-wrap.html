Buffalo Chicken Wrap

Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2 | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 large tortillas
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/4 cup buffalo sauce
  • Lettuce and ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Toss chicken in buffalo sauce.
  2. Layer tortilla with lettuce, chicken, cheese, and ranch.
  3. Roll tightly and cut in half.
